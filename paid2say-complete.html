🔓 50-Referral Gateway

Your first 50 referrals count toward Year 1's 100. This milestone proves you're serious and puts you on the Legacy track.

💵 Same Commission Rate

Residuals pay the exact same rate as standard commissions. A sale is a sale — whether it's their first visit or their fiftieth.

🛡️ One Grace Year

Drop below 36 referrals one year? No penalty. Life happens. Your residuals stay active.

⚠️ Probation

Two consecutive years below 36? Residuals pause. Account enters probation in the background.

🔄 Re-Qualify

Hit 100 referrals in 3 of the next 4 years to restore Legacy status and reactivate residuals.

📋 Direct Referrals Only

You earn on clients YOU brought in. One level deep. No downstream chains, no MLM. You eat what you kill.

BANKING & INFRASTRUCTURE
BANKING

How the Money Moves

Cash App-style internal ledger. Built on banking-as-a-service infrastructure.

✅ RECOMMENDED

Stripe Treasury + Stripe Issuing

FDIC-insured accounts (via Goldman Sachs / Evolve Bank) embedded in the platform. Virtual + physical branded Paid2Say cards. This is how Cash App, Shopify Balance, and Lyft payouts work.
✓ FDIC insured · ✓ Virtual & physical cards · ✓ Internal transfers free · ✓ Compliance handled
✗ Requires Stripe approval · ✗ Revenue share on interchange
ALTERNATIVE

Unit (Banking-as-a-Service)

Purpose-built BaaS for fintechs. Real bank accounts + debit cards via partner banks. Designed specifically for marketplace platforms like Paid2Say.
✓ Built for this use case · ✓ White-label cards · ✓ ACH + book transfers
✗ Monthly platform fees · ✗ Smaller ecosystem
FUTURE PHASE

Money Transmitter License (MTL)

At 100K+ users, apply for your own license. Full control, no revenue share. $500K-$2M+ setup, 12-18 months. Use BaaS partners until then.
✓ Full control · ✓ Maximum margins
✗ Expensive · ✗ Heavy compliance burden
