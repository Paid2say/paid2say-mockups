What Is Legacy?

Most affiliate programs pay you once and forget you exist. Paid2Say is different.

Legacy is our residual commission program for affiliates who prove they're serious. When a customer you referred keeps coming back to a business — you keep getting paid. Same rate. Every time.

You brought them in. You should benefit when they come back.

But Legacy isn't handed out. It's earned. Through consistent referrals and sustained effort. Think of it like building equity — your voice appreciates in value the more you use it.
