🏦 How the Banking Side Works

Building a "Cash App-style" ledger system. Here are the real options.

✅ RECOMMENDED

Stripe Treasury + Stripe Issuing

Stripe Treasury gives you actual FDIC-insured bank accounts (via partner banks like Goldman Sachs / Evolve Bank) embedded in your platform. Stripe Issuing lets you create virtual + physical Paid2Say branded cards. This is literally how Cash App, Shopify Balance, and Lyft driver payouts work.
✓ FDIC insured · ✓ Virtual & physical cards · ✓ Internal transfers = free · ✓ Well-documented API · ✓ Compliance handled
✗ Requires Stripe approval (takes weeks) · ✗ Revenue share on interchange
ALTERNATIVE

Unit (Banking-as-a-Service)

Purpose-built BaaS platform. Creates real bank accounts and debit cards for your users via partner banks (Blue Ridge Bank, Choice Financial). Built specifically for fintechs and marketplaces. Faster approval than Stripe Treasury in some cases.
✓ Designed for this exact use case · ✓ White-label cards · ✓ ACH, wires, book transfers · ✓ Joint compliance
✗ Monthly platform fees · ✗ Smaller ecosystem than Stripe
ALTERNATIVE

Marqeta + Partner Bank

Marqeta powers the cards for Cash App, DoorDash, Instacart, and Square. Best-in-class card issuing with real-time controls. Pair with a partner bank (Sutton Bank, Celtic Bank) for the accounts.
✓ Used by Cash App literally · ✓ Real-time card controls · ✓ Massive scale proven
✗ More complex setup · ✗ Need separate bank partnership · ✗ Higher minimums
FUTURE PHASE

Apply for Money Transmitter License (MTL)

If Paid2Say scales large enough, you'd eventually want your own MTL — but that's a state-by-state process ($$$, lawyers, compliance). For now, the BaaS partners above handle this for you under their licenses. Revisit at 100K+ users.
✓ Full control · ✓ No revenue share · ✓ Your own bank rails
✗ $500K–$2M+ to set up · ✗ 12–18 months · ✗ Ongoing compliance burden
